Frequently asked questions

Will checking my rate hurt my credit?

No. Getting a personalized quote uses a soft credit pull. If you proceed, a hard pull may be required.

Do you work with first-time buyers?

Absolutely. We’ll explore low down payment programs, down-payment assistance, and lock protection.

What documents will I need?

Typically 2 years of W-2s or tax returns, recent pay stubs, bank statements, and ID. Specifics vary by program.
