Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our dental services.

We recommend visiting every six months for a routine cleaning and checkup. However, some patients may need more frequent visits depending on their oral health needs. Your dentist will advise you on the best schedule for your situation.

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team will help you understand your coverage and submit claims on your behalf. We also offer flexible payment options for patients without insurance.

For severe pain, trauma, or swelling, call us immediately at (604) 555-1234. We reserve same-day appointments for emergencies. If it's after hours, follow the instructions on our voicemail to reach the on-call dentist.

When performed under professional supervision, teeth whitening is very safe. We use clinically proven products that minimize sensitivity while delivering excellent results. We'll evaluate your teeth first to ensure whitening is appropriate for you.

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last 20-30 years or even a lifetime. They have a success rate of over 95% and are the most durable tooth replacement option available today.
